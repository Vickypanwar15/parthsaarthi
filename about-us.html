About ParthSaarthi

ParthSaarthi ek student guidance platform hai jo college students ko unke career decisions me clarity dene ke liye banaya gaya hai.

Is naam ke peeche soch kya hai?

Mahabharat me, jab Arjun (Parth) yudh ke maidan me confused tha, tab Krishna ne use sirf yudh nahi, balki jeevan ka marg dikhaya.

Aaj ke samay me college students bhi Arjun jaise hi hote hain — confused, pressure me, aur direction ki talash me.

ParthSaarthi ka matlab hai: student ke rath ka saarthi — jo use sahi disha me guide kare.

Ye website kyun banayi gayi?

College life ke dauran students ko ye lagta hai ki: sirf degree aur syllabus hi kaafi hoga. Lekin graduation ke baad jab job ka sawal aata hai, tab confusion aur regret shuru hota hai.

Ye website isliye banayi gayi hai taaki:

  • Students ko early stage me clarity mile
  • Unhe pata ho ki kya padhna chahiye aur kya nahi
  • Private aur government dono options clear ho
  • Career decisions movies ke dialogue par nahi, reality par based ho

ParthSaarthi ka approach

  • No fake motivation
  • No guaranteed success claims
  • Only practical guidance
  • Experience + reality based content

Ye platform students ko ye nahi kehta: “Sab kuch chhod do, passion follow karo.”
Balki ye kehta hai: “Passion ke saath planning bhi zaroori hai.”

“Karm karo, phal ki chinta mat karo.”
— Bhagavad Gita

Agar ParthSaarthi kisi ek student ko bhi graduation se pehle sahi direction de paata hai, to ye platform apna purpose poora kar leta hai.

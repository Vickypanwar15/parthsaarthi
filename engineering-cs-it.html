ParthSaarthi Har Student ka Marg-Darshan

Engineering Students ke liye Career Reality

Computer Science / Information Technology

Ye guide 1st aur 2nd year ke CS/IT students ke liye hai jo apni degree ke saath real career banana chahte hain.

Is guide ka purpose kis ke liye hai?

Ye career guide specially Tier-2 aur Tier-3 colleges ke Computer Science / Information Technology students ke liye banayi gayi hai.

IIT, NIT aur top institutes ke students ko generally:

  • Strong peer group milta hai
  • Better exposure aur guidance milti hai
  • Industry ke saath early connect hota hai

Lekin Tier-2 aur Tier-3 colleges ke students aksar:

  • Proper career guidance se door rehte hain
  • College syllabus par zyada dependent ho jaate hain
  • Late realise karte hain ki job ke liye kya chahiye

ParthSaarthi ka uddeshya ye hai ki:

  • Tier-2 / Tier-3 college ke students ko bhi wahi clarity mile
  • Jo clarity top institutes ke students ko milti hai
  • Taaki degree ke baad regret na ho

Ye guide kisi college ya student ko judge karne ke liye nahi, balki un students ke liye hai jinke paas direction nahi hai, lekin mehnat karne ka jazba hai.

1. College ke bharose mat raho

College syllabus aur corporate world me bahut difference hota hai. Skills bina self-learning ke develop nahi hoti.

2. Pehle decide karo – kya tumhe job chahiye?

Graduation ke baad offer letter na hone ka dard sirf wahi samajh sakta hai jo us phase se guzra ho. Isliye 1st ya 2nd year me hi decision lena zaroori hai.

3. Core subjects strong karo (Must for all)

  • Operating System
  • DBMS
  • Data Structures
  • OOPs
  • Computer Networks
  • Compiler Design (basic understanding)

4. Private sector job ke liye kya padhna chahiye?

4.1 Programming Language (Ek choose karo)

  • Java (Backend ke liye best)
  • Python
  • C / C++

4.2 Coding & Problem Solving

  • Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA)
  • Arrays, Strings, Linked List, Stack, Queue
  • Basic Recursion & Searching

Practice platforms: LeetCode, GeeksForGeeks, NeetCode

4.3 Aptitude & Communication

  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • Reasoning
  • Basic English communication

4.4 Projects & Internship

  • College ke projects copy mat karo
  • Khud ka mini project banao
  • Internship apply karo (even unpaid)

5. Government job (IT Officer) ke liye kya padhna chahiye?

CS/IT students ke liye bank aur PSU sector me achhi opportunities hoti hain.

5.1 Core Technical Subjects

  • Operating System
  • DBMS
  • Data Structures
  • Computer Networks
  • Software Engineering

5.2 Banking + General Subjects

  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • Reasoning
  • English
  • Basic Banking Awareness

Example exam: IBPS SO (IT Officer)
Prelims: Oct / Nov
Mains + Interview: Dec
